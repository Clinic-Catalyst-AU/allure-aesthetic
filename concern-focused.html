A different way of looking

When you look in the mirror in the morning you do not see a wrinkle. You see your face. So why is every clinic offering to treat one line, one syringe, one panicked appointment at a time? Your face is a whole. Allure treats it that way.

01
The Foundation

Skin first.

Before volume. Before threads. Before a single unit of anything. We build the skin. Texture. Hydration. Tone. The layer that catches the light. If the canvas is wrong, every treatment after looks wrong.

Skin needling, NCTF bio-revitalisation, chemical peels, LED. Sequenced over weeks. By the second month the skin starts reflecting light again. By the third, the face under it is ready for what comes next.

02
The Architecture

Volume, where the
face lives.

The face deflates before it falls. A few millilitres of structure restored, placed precisely where time removed it, and the architecture comes back. Cheek. Temple. Tear trough. Lip. Done with restraint so the result reads as rested, never altered.

Jamie places small amounts at a time and reviews you back two weeks later. Layered, not loaded. The face you had at thirty-eight, looking back.

03
The Lift

Definition returned.

PDO threads, placed beneath the skin, lift the tissue immediately and stimulate collagen for the months that follow. The jawline sharpens. The brow returns to where it was. The neck softens its forward pull. No surgery. No general anaesthetic. A day of careful rest and the rest of your life looking like you.

Thread lifts are the treatment most clinics in the eastern corridor do not do well. Jamie places them daily.
